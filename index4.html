The Faculty of "Media communications and multimedia technologies" of DSTU was founded on March 16, 2012 on the basis of the department "Mass communications and multimedia technologies", established by the university together with the Media group "South Region" on November 30, 2010.
Our goal is to develop new markets for end-to-end technologies and creative industries through the formation of responsible, creative, proactive and ready for continuous self-development professionals in media communications, multimedia technologies, creativity and art management. In the process of training future professionals in the field of multimedia, the faculty uses the latest educational technologies. The classrooms of the faculty are equipped with modern multimedia equipment and computers with specialized software for working with video, graphics, sound, 2D and 3D animation. The practice of students begins from the first day of training - regional, city and local Internet projects are integrated into the activities of the faculty. Among them are the on-air TV channel "TNT4", the radio station "Rostov FM" (89.4), the information and entertainment portal rostovlife.ru. Thus, students of the Faculty of MKMT can realize themselves as videographers, video engineers, sound engineers, layout designers, journalists, animators, producers, advertising and public relations specialists.
